Masala Chai

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 tsp Tea Leaves
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Ginger (crushed)
  • 2-3 Cardamom pods

Steps:

  1. Boil water with ginger & cardamom.
  2. Add tea leaves and sugar, let it simmer.

  4. Strain into cups and serve hot.
